Stage IIIB — Dual-Arm, Double-Blind HMAC Deletion Challenge (Context)

High-level design summary of Stage IIIB used to establish large-scale, randomized negative controls and cross-operator independence before Stage IV. This figure is context-only; operational plans and execution logs remain governed and archived.

2Double-Blind, HMAC-Randomized Two-Arm Challenge
Each arm begins as a blinded baseline and branches into (i) a positive-control re-export and (ii) an HMAC-randomized deletion working copy. Operators alternate arms and are blinded to both file identity and deletion pattern.
